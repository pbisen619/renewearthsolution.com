Our Services

Waste Collection

Waste collection for industrial, commercial, and residential
sources involves collecting waste from its source,
transporting it, and disposing of it.
Waste can be collected using curbside pickup or drop-off points.

Scanning & Identification

Our AI-driven system categorizes waste in real-time using advanced image recognition technology.

Sanitization & Processing

We ensure safe decontamination of waste through advanced sanitization techniques, prioritizing safety measures in waste treatment.

Material Separation

Our process efficiently separates different waste materials, directing them towards appropriate recycling pathways.

Granules Production

We transform waste materials into reusable granules, contributing to a circular economy.
